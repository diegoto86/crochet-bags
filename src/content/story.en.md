Lola Faletti begins in Lima, with a woman who continues creating from experience.

Not from nostalgia. Not from haste. From the judgment of someone who has lived, observed, and learned to do things with patience.

Each bag comes from that mature way of creating: with time, with character, and with a beauty that does not need excess.

From Barcelona, that energy becomes visual direction and a point of view ready to travel further.

Experience can also create the future.
